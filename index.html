Our story

Founded by Ben Corso, BAC Business Consulting brings over 30 years of experience in allied health practice ownership. Our journey started in 1996 within the field of physiotherapy; like many of you, we had to learn the business side of health the hard way.

Starting with no formal business plan and managing the books after hours, our team eventually grew "The Physio Clinic" from a single room to a multi-site, multi-disciplinary operation generating over $4.5M in revenue.

In 2021, we successfully navigated the sale of the business to Australia’s largest integrated Allied Health Group. This transition led to Regional and General Management roles overseeing 30+ clinics and $40M+ in revenue. Today, we leverage that collective experience to help you achieve the same success.
