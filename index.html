My Story

Founded by Ben Corso, BAC Business Consulting brings over 30 years of experience in allied health practice ownership. My journey started in 1996 as a physiotherapist, but like many of you, I had to learn business the hard way.

Starting with no business plan and doing the books at night, I eventually grew "The Physio Clinic" from a single room to a multi-site, multi-disciplinary operation generating over $2.2M in revenue.

In 2021, I successfully sold the business to Australia’s largest integrated Allied Health Group and transitioned into a General Manager role overseeing 30+ clinics. Now, I use that experience to help you achieve the same success.
